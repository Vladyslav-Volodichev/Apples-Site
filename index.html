Apples

Apples are one of the most common types of fruits. They are rich in vitamins and nutrients and taste good. The food life of apples depends on their variety, storage method and environmental conditions. Apples can be stored in the refrigerator for up to several months, and at room temperature - from several days to several weeks. To extend the shelf life, it is necessary to avoid damage to the surface of the apples and store them in a cool and dry place. Apples are available for purchase at most grocery stores and supermarkets all year round.

Red Delicious

This is one of the most common and well-known varieties of apples. It has a beautiful bright red skin and a sweet, crunchy taste.

Golden Delicious

This is another very popular variety of apples. It usually has a yellow skin and a sweet, mild taste.

Gala

This is a relatively new variety of apples that was developed in New Zealand. It has a beautiful striped peel and a sweet, juicy taste.

Fuji

This is a variety of apples that was developed in Japan. It has a firm skin and a sweet, crunchy taste.

Honeycrisp

This is a relatively new variety of apples that was developed in the United States. It has a sweet, crunchy taste and has a good stored ability.
